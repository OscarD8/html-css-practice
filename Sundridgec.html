St Mary's Sundridge

Welcome to our lovely church of St Mary's, Sundridge in the parish of Sundridge with Ide Hill and Toys Hill. We look forward to welcoming you at a service soon.

Services are held at Sundridge @ 10.30am every first and third Sunday in the month.

Services are held at Ide Hill @ 10.30am every second and fourth Sunday in the month

Services are held at Toys Hill @ 09.00am every first Sunday in the month.

On the 5th Sunday the service alternates between Sundridge and Ide Hill @ 10.30am.

All are welcome at our services
